Technology - Tips To Protect From Phone Addiction.

Lets ask some hard questions.

Do you feel like there is a little too much technology in your life? Or do you feel that you have developed a slight addiction? Maybe you would like a cure for your troubles? Well, you're in luck I will present you with some tipis on protecting yourself from these addictive apps and practices. Below are some good ways to fight the man.

  • Tip 1: Disable all notification for noncritical applications, as these are distractive traps with a goal to keep your attention.
  • Tip 2: Delete useless apps that provide little to your life. This is simple if it's not there it won't distract you.
  • Tip 3: Reduce the severity of notifications. You donâ€™t need an alert every time an artist puts out a new song.
  • Tip 4: Hide distracting apps in folders far out of reach, or better yet delete then if they only provide a distraction to you.
  • Tip 5: Turn on gray mode to reduce the color and therefore the attractiveness of your phone and apps.
  • Tip 6: Make your phone boring, keep only productive apps on there. This means no games or social media. This removes the phone from your list of entertainment sources.
  • Tip 7: make your phone as minimalistic as possible by disabling unnecessary widgets and plugins that complicate your experience.
  • Tip 8: To protect your privacy walk through every menu in the settings of your phone to disable sharing and app permissions.

While there is definitely more that you can do and you certainly don't have to do all of this. I feel like this a good way to start if you so do wish.
